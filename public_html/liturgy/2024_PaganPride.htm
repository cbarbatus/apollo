INITIATING THE RITE

Bards: Sing Let It Grow (Courtesy of Dr. Suesss The Lorax) as the folk gather

Paul: A Blessed EvenTide to you all. I am Sun Dragon, your haphazard host for this ritual. Friends, To whom henceforth and solely for the duration of this ritual shall be known as Druids, In honor of Earth Day, Welcome to the Ravens Cry Grove, ADF presentation of our Earth Mother Rite at Pagan Community Retreat.

And now, let me introduce our Senior Druid and my co-host for the evening, Lisa

Senior Druid Lisa:

Tonight our ritual will honor deities of the Irish Pantheon. Although our Groves ritual center is a Celtic one - we also celebrate various High Days within the context of other cultures from the Indo-European language family.

The focus of our ritual tradition is building relationships with the Kindreds. The Kindred are The Ancestors, those we all know. The Nature Spirits, of leaf and flower, fin, feather, and fur. And the Shining ones, what we call the Deities. And we do this through reciprocity. We will be making many offerings and invocations to the Kindreds to join us here. The invocation and offering is a time of thanks and Praise only. Please do not make any requests at this time. Later, when the Blessing Cup comes around, this will be your opportunity to ask the Kindreds for something in return - so please, hold on to those thoughts until then.

When we come around with the Blessing Cup, it will hold Chaucers Mead, a California, Irish tradition, mead. You may partake of the Blessing Cup by either holding it to your head or your chest, pouring a small amount on the ground or you may imbibe directly from our offering cup to accept the Waters of Life. If there is anyone here that wants to drink but is uncomfortable with drinking from the cup, let us know and we can give you a small paper cup to drink from.

And to let you know, there is a Gaelic word you will be hearing us use over and over. That word is Bodh s Amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/ and it means May it be so, or as our Wiccan friends like to say, So Mote It Be.

And with that, we shall begin:

Take another deep breath and feel your connection to Her in the pull of our sacred space, calling you, whispering on the wind.

In this space we create our sacred realities. We can become all the Kindreds believe us to be.

HONORING THE EARTH MOTHER

Angela:

First and foremost, we honor the Earth Mother, the All Mother;

For it is she from whom we have all emerged,

It is she who sustains us,

It is she to whom, in the fullness of time,

Our bones will return.

 

Come, Blessed Goddess, and hear our prayer;

We ask that you support and surround us

For this rite, as you do for all rites,

For this day, as you do for all days.

 

Eldest of all beings, crowned with every grace,

We give you honor, and we give you praise;

Yours is the joy of the rain, and of every sacred thing.

We honor you best when we walk in balance;

We honor you best when we make our footprints light;

We honor you best when we become your true champions.

 

Earth Mother, All Mother, Our Mother,

Ignite your spark within us

That we may know ourselves to be truly human,

Truly holy, and truly part of the web of life.

Join us in the warmth and light of our Good Fire.

We welcome you here this night.

STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

Paul:

We gather between the Earth and Sky
to worship and honor the Kindreds as one people.
To sing their praise and make our offerings
as we seek their blessings in return.
As our ancestors did and our children may do, so do we now!

Earth Day is in a little over a week, and so it is appropriate that we set aside this time to honor the Earth Mother. We shall honor her through reciprocity - which means showing our gratitude for her gifts to us, by giving her gifts in return through offerings, so that she may give to us again; we will also be honoring the resources shes provides us and how we make use of them in our lives; and we will do a working to connect us to our Mother Earth - while contemplating what it means to be her child.

We likewise express our intention that all the Kindreds the Ancestors, the Nature Spirits, and the Shining Ones also join with us this night, as the Earth Mother is mother to us all.

Bodh s amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

KEY OFFERINGS

Main Offering

Paul:

Earth Mother, sustainer of all creation, from you flows all that we need to live, to work, to love, to play. Your blessed waters are a joy to behold, not only do they quench our thirst, but they also provide us nourishment, entertainment, transportation, and beauty in their very existence. From the smallest creek to the largest ocean, your liquid blessings are truly The Waters of Life! Your vast expanses of land, from verdant meadow to windswept crag, are home to countless creatures, great and small. You support us and surround us with the means to feed and clothe ourselves, and provide us with all we need to feed those other parts of us as well pigments to paint with, clay to sculpt with, wood and stone to carve, fibers to craft with, and all that allows us to conduct our business and build community together. The winds of your sacred breath that blow across the plains and the fields, that swirl among the clouds, carry the seeds of new life, lift us up like birds on the wing, sing the songs of a myriad voices, and bring back the blessed waters in the endless cycle of the pouring out of your blessings

We honor you as the great Provider, she who gives all that we may live. We honor the sacrifice you make to sustain us all. May we use these resources wisely; may we remember to use them for the good of all; may we revel in the beauty and joy that they bring. Let us tend well your land and sea and sky that we return those blessings unto you.

With love, we present you an offering of seeds native to this land, and cool, pure water.

(make offering of seed and water)

Earth Mother, accept our offering!

CALL FOR THE BLESSINGS

 

Paul:

 

Druids, all our offerings, all we have done so far in our rite, have led to this moment. This is magic time! We have made offerings to the Kindreds, we have honored our Earth Mother, and now we shall ask for their blessings in return. Visualize the blessings that each of you desire and need from the Kindreds. See it clearly in your minds eye. And when we ask for the Waters of Life, when we say, "Give us the Waters of Life", see the blessing you desire descend into the upraised cup like a mist, filling the cup. And when we drink of the Waters, you will receive your blessing. So close your eyes and take a moment to visualize your needs, and the needs of our Community, that we may all receive the blessings we desire.

[Allow time for the people to meditate on needs and desires.]
