INITIATING THE RITE

Darkness. No procession. Folk are already assembled in a circle around the sacred space.

 

Guardian

 

Angela: Druids, we gather together in darkness for this winter solstice celebration. As in elder days, we have watched as the nights have consumed the days and the sun has grown distant in the cold and yonder sky. The fields have fallowed, the beasts have burrowed, and the trees have shed their summer shade in favor of a long winter’s nap. But on this solstice night, a spark flickers. A kindling light twinkles on the horizon as Baldr [BALD-er] stirs. The longest night and darkest hour are behind us now, and we turn our gaze toward the west, as a newborn sun begins its slow and steady journey, returning to its full power. Hail, Sol, driver of the day-star chariot! On this night, let us fill our hearts with anticipation, and fill this space with merriment as we sing, dance, and encourage the return of the light.

 

Lisa: Druids! Welcome to the Yule ritual of Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF!  We stand here at the threshold, yearning for the sun’s return. As we open our rite, we invite you to call on the spirits of joy and wonder. Friends, if you wish, place a tickle in your hearts and a twinkle in your eyes as we invite the elves, the dwarves, the fae, and the Nisse [NEE-suh] to join us in our gaiety. Warrior, commence the Hammer Rite.


Hammer Rite

Warrior:
Stand to one direction, holding the hammer out
         Hammer of Fire, Hallow and Hold this Holy-stead
Turn to opposite direction, holding the hammer out
         Hammer of Ice, Hallow and hold this Holy-Stead

Holding the hammer up
         Hammer over me, hallow and hold this sacred stead.
Holding the hammer down
         Hammer under me, hallow and hold this sacred stead.

Replace hammer

         May the gods guide us,

May our oaths keep us,

May our deeds free us,

May our ancestors aid us always,

May the gods banish from this land and space all evil and wrong,

Hallow this place, and shield from this area all baneful wights!

Let the gods’ blessings be over our heads.

 

Outdwellers

 

Angela: Druids, as we prepare to make offering to the Outdwellers, please avert your eyes so as not to form a relationship with them, and also look to the Outdwellers in your own hearts.  This is a celebration of joy.  All that we carry into the sacred space today will flower.  Take only that which you wish to root, grow, and flourish.

 

Warrior!  Make the offering and appease the Outdwellers who do not support our ways.
