Raven's Cry Grove- Yule 2014 (Norse)

Written by Anya and Jackie Bushong

 

For Ritual January 3, 2015

 

 

INITIATING THE RITE

The folk assemble outside the nemeton to prepare

Make PRE-RITUAL ANNOUNCEMENTS

 

Guardian

Mike: Druids!  Welcome to the Yule ritual of Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF!  We stand here at the threshold.  Warrior, call a guardian for our rite.

 

Heather M.:  [Standing in the North]

Mighty Thor, protector of the Gods,

Wielder of the mighty hammer Mjolnir. [MULE near]

We come together in this place

To worship in the old ways.

We would ask you to protect this space.

To dispatch all enemies of Asgard  [Az-gard],

Any other energies which may not support our work here,

We offer you Ale so you may not thirst.

So it must be!

(Offering of Ale)

 

Anya: Here we create our threshold.  We open the roadway to the sacred center.  Warrior, mark this space that the outdwellers shall not enter.

 

Warrior draws a line at the entrance.

Heather M.: Thor - we ask you to watch this threshold.

 

Outdwellers

Mike: Druids, as we prepare to make offering to the Outdwellers, please avert your eyes so as not to form a relationship with them, and also look to the Outdwellers in your own hearts.  This is a celebration of joy.  All that we carry into the nemeton today will flower.  Take only that which you wish to root, grow, and flourish.

Warrior!  Make offering and appease the Outdwellers who do not support our ways.

 

 

Heather M.:

Mighty spirits of chaos,

dwellers of the outer dark,

who have stood against the gods.

You who care not for our ways,

known and unknown to us as Other.

Ancient dark ones,

take this offering in honor and respect

and trouble not our working here this day.

Warrior makes an offering to the Outdwellers,

So it must be!

 

Procession and Purification

Anya:  Druids release the cares of your mundane life.

Take a deep breath in and allow your mind to be still.

Thoughts outside of this time are released.

You are here spiraling ever forward in the present.

Feel the pull of our sacred space,

calling you, whispering on the wind.

In this space we create our sacred realities.

We can become all the Kindreds believe us to be.

Purification allows us to transform into our sacred selves.

Please come forward, be purified,

and attune yourself to mythic time.

Let go and cross the divide.

 

Procession – Song: The Holly and the Ivy- Traditional

The holly and the ivy,

When they are both full grown

Of all the trees that are in the wood

The holly bears the crown

O the rising of the sun

And the running of the deer

The playing of the merry organ

Sweet singing of the choir

 

Folk are censed and aspersed as they enter the space.

When all folk are in place,

 

Anya: By the light of the fire and the might of the water, make this Grove be whole and holy.

 

 

Welcome

Mike: ring chime 3x3 times saying:

Ancestors! (chime 3x)

Nature Spirits! (chime 3x)

Shining Ones! (chime 3x)

We have crossed the threshold into sacred space.

Please reconnect with the sacred cosmos as we prepare to open the gates to the otherworlds,

the place where the kindreds will reveal themselves once more. We come tonight to honor the Wild Hunt, and to gather in fellowship to feast on this dark Winter’s eve.

 

Kaila: Earth Mother- Jord  [YORD]

Ah,  Jord! We sing your praises.

Your very touch causes trees to fruit and seeds to sprout.

Goddess of the uncivilized, uncultivated, wild earth,

You who were Odin’s first consort,

You who is the life-giving force of the planet,

Great giantess of lush and life-giving form,

Great Earth Mother we do give you honor this eve.

Jord, accept our offering.

(Offering of fruit or vegetable seeds)

So it must be!

 

Opening Statement

 

Mike:  Today we gather between the Earth and Sky.

To worship and honor the Kindred as one people.

To sing their praise and make our sacrifice.

As we seek their blessings in return.

 

Anya:   As we have passed solstice, the days will begin to lengthen. Yet we are still in midst of the cold grasp of winter, and it will be many weeks before the dark has released its hold. In this time when the cold winds blow and the nights are dark and feral, it is customary for the folk to gather and share tales and feast. For this is the time of the Wild Hunt, and it is wise to stay indoors when the ghostly spectacle is afoot. Let us celebrate the light and life within and toast our deities and our kindred! Let us come together as a community and revel in this time of coming together.

 

 

Mike: So, let us pray with a good fire

As our ancestors did and our children may do,

so do we now - that our ancestors may know

that the old ways are not forgotten:

 

We are here to honor the Old Bargain

between mortals and the Kindreds.

So let us join together as one folk

to make our offerings in joy and reverence.

 

So it must be.

All: So it must be. 

 

Inspiration

Harp music to accompany the inspiration invocation

 

Mike: Bragi,  great god of harpists and poets.  We ask you for inspiration for our rite!

 

Russell:

Great and wise Bragi [BRAH-yee],

From you comes great fluency of speech and skill with words.

First maker of poetry, son of Odin, long-bearded god,

Bragi from whom the Bragnungs are sprung,

Come now to bring peace to all gathered,

May our songs give you praise,

Oh, Bragi, Golden Tongued one, listen as we play for you, in your honor.

(Offering of Song)(harp solo)

Bragi, Accept our offering.

All: Bragi, accept our offering.

So it must be.

 

Establish the Group Mind

Anya: [Two powers meditation]

 

Recreate the Cosmos

Mike: Druids, let us now re-establish the Sacred Center of the Worlds.

 

 

The Three Realms

Anya:  The waters support and surround us.

The land extends about us.

The sky stretches above us.

And the center burns a living flame.

Let us pray with a good fire.

May all the Kindred bless us.

May our worship be true.

May our actions be just.

May our love be pure.

Blessings and honor and worship to the holy ones.

 

 

Well, Fire, and Tree

(Three Druids come forward and go to their chosen hallow.  They will remain until all hallows are honored)

 

Well

Ken: Great Mimir’s [MEE mirs] well, fountain of all wit and wisdom.

You from which we receive inspiration and eloquence.

You to whom Odin gave an eye for one draught.

We make this sacrifice to you,

That you may carry our sacrifice to those to whom we send.

To those of this world and the worlds below.”

So it must be!

(Offering of Silver)

 

Fire

Heather G.: Great flame which warms and protects us.

You which combines with the air and water

To create Bifrost [Bay-vrost]  the rainbow bridge.

We make this sacrifice to you,

That you may carry our sacrifice to those to whom we send.

To those of this world and the worlds above.”

So it must be!

(Offering of Oil)

 

Tree

Addy: Great Yggdrasil [IG-drah-sil], World tree.

You whose roots reach down below the lowest world.

You whose crown reaches above the highest world.

You who touches all the worlds.

We make this sacrifice to you,

That you may carry our sacrifice to those to whom we send.

To those of this world, the worlds below and the words above.

So it must be!

(Offering of Water)

 

 

OPEN THE GATES

 

Opening the Gates

 

Mike: “Now that we are centered and prepared and our hallows are ready, let us now call upon our Gatekeeper to open the gates by which we make offering.”

 

Heather M.:

[INVOCATION]

“Heimdall [HAME-dall], Gatekeeper, Son of 9 mothers.

You who stands watch at Bifrost [BAY vrust]

Keenly listening, forever watching

We call upon you to open the gates so that our word and actions may be heard and felt.

(Offering of Mead)

 

[OPENING THE GATES]

 

Heimdall [HAME-dall], we call on you to make this well Mimir’s[MEE mirs (roll the r)].

Heimdall, we call on you to make this fire Bifrost [BAY vr-ust].

Heimdall, we call on you to make this tree Yggdrasil  [IG drah-sil].

 

Heimdall, let the gates be open!” (3 times)

So it must be.

 

All:  Repeat

 

Mike: Druids, we are now at the center of the cosmos.  We are woven into the sacred fabric of the universe.  Here, the Kindreds can hear our thoughts and know our hearts.  Let there only be truth here.

 

 

INVITING THE THREE KINDREDS

 

Anya: “We now take this time to make our offerings to the Kindreds – the Ancient Ones, the Noble Ones, and the Shining Ones.

 

Mike: First, we will make our offering and invitation to the Ancestors.

 

Ancestors

 

 

Ken:

“Ancestors, Wise Mothers, Great Fathers.

Those recently passed, and those forgotten in time.

You who have shown us the right and the wrong.

We ask you to send your thoughts up from the depths of Helheim [HELL hame].

We ask you to send your thoughts down from Vallhalla in Asgard [Azgard].

We ask that you join us in our rite and lead us in the ways of old.

Ancestors, join us in our rite.”

(offering of mead)

 

Nature spirits

 

Jessica: “As told in tales of long ago,

An enemy was trying to invade the land.

They tried to invade from the South,

But a great bull rushed down,

Followed by all the cloven hoofed animals of the land.

Next they tried to invade from the East,

But a hill giant rushed down,

Followed by all the creatures of bark and leaf, of soil and crystal.

Next they tried to invade from the North,

But a bellowing dragon rushed down,

Followed by all the reptiles and creatures of scale.

Next they tried to invade from the West,

But a giant eagle rushed down,

Followed by all the creatures of feather.

And the invasion never happened.

Landvættir [lan-VAY-tear], protectors of the land, we honor you,

And welcome you to join us at our rite.

Landvættir [lan-VAY-tear], join us in our rite.”

(Offering of meat)

 

Deities

Darin: “Aesir [AY-seer], Vanir [VA-neer], gods and goddesses of the nine worlds.

You who will fight among the mighty dead at Ragnarok.

We honor you here.

Gods, Patrons, and spirit allies of all present,

Powers of this place known to us and unknown,

We would offer you like honor.

Aesir, Vanir, join us in our rite.”

(offering of special scented oil)

 

Praise offerings

Mike: “Let all who would offer praise and thanks to their gods or allies come forth and do so now.”

 

Re-centering of the Folk and the Gathered Powers

…Now with the all assembled here in this sacred place, we call upon and honor the patron powers of this rite.

 

Calling Upon the Patron Powers of the Rite: Odin / Freya / Thor

 

Odin

From the far, ancient lands of our people,

Chief of the Shining Ones

We call to thee across all of time

And all the words of the Gods.

Thy people are still here, O wise one.

Come to us again, and give us to drink of thy

Cauldron of life and of inspiration

That we may prosper once again.

Come to us now and be with us here

To celebrate this joyous season.

Hail Odin!

Freya

Be with us here, in this rite

To celebrate this joyous season.

Hail Freya!

Thor
