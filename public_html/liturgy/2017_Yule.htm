Raven's Cry Grove- Yule 2018 (Norse)
Written by Jackie
 
For Ritual January 6, 2018
 

Make PRE-RITUAL ANNOUNCEMENTS in the walkway between the Sanctuary and the Dining area, or in the dining area if it is too cold.

 

Assemble outside the hof to prepare. ( Old Norse makes the distinction between hof "a hall, a sanctuary with a roof" and hǫrgr "an altar, any cult site without a roof". The prevalent meaning of hof in Old Norse literature is "temple, sanctuary")

 

Altar Keeper:  Mike


 
INITIATING THE RITE 7pm Sharp

Guardian
Jackie: Druids! Welcome all who honor the Kindred with us this night!  We stand here at the threshold.  Warrior, call a guardian for our rite.
 
Elizabeth: [Standing in the North]
Mighty Thor, protector of the Gods,
wielder of the mighty hammer Mjolnir. [MULE near]
We come together in this place
To worship in the old ways.
We would ask you to protect this space.
To dispatch all enemies of Asgard [Az-gard],
Any other energies which may not support our work here,
We offer you Ale so you may not thirst.
So it must be!
(Offering of ale)
 
Jackie: Here we create our threshold.  We open the roadway to the sacred center.  Warrior, mark this space that the outdwellers shall not enter.
 
Warrior draws a line at the entrance.
Elizabeth: Thor - we ask you to watch this threshold.
 
Outdwellers
Jackie: Druids, as we prepare to make offering to the Outdwellers, please avert your eyes so as not to form a relationship with them.  Also look to the outdwellers in your own hearts.  This is a celebration of joy.  All that we carry into the hof today will flower.  Take only that which you wish to root, grow, and flourish.
Warrior!  Make offering and appease the Outdwellers who do not support our ways.
 
Elizabeth:
Mighty spirits of chaos,
dwellers of the outer dark,
who have stood against the gods.
You who care not for our ways,
known and unknown to us as Other.
Ancient dark ones,
take this offering in honor and respect
and trouble not our working here this day.
Warrior makes an offering of whiskey to the Outdwellers,
So it must be!
 
Procession and Purification
Jackie:  Druids release the cares of your mundane life.
Take a deep breath in and allow your mind to be still.
Thoughts outside of this time are released.
Feel the pull of our sacred space,
calling you, whispering on the wind.
In this space we create our sacred realities.
Purification allows us to transform into our sacred selves.
Please come forward, be purified,
and attune yourself to mythic time.
Let go and cross the divide.

Opening Statement
 
Ken:  Today we gather between the Earth and Sky.
To worship and honor the Kindred as one people.
To sing their praise and make our sacrifice.
As we seek their blessings in return.
 
Jackie:   As we have passed solstice, the days will begin to lengthen. Yet we are still in midst of the cold grasp of winter, and it will be many weeks before the dark has released its hold. In this time when the cold winds blow and the nights are dark and feral, it is customary for the folk to gather and share tales and feast.

 

Let us celebrate the light and life within and toast our deities and our kindred! Let us gather as a community and revel in this time of coming together.
 
Ken: So, let us pray with a good fire

so do we now - that our ancestors may know
that the old ways are not forgotten:
 
We are here to honor the Old Bargain
between mortals and the Kindred.
So let us join together as one folk
to make our offerings in joy and reverence.
 
So it must be.
